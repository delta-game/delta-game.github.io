DELTΔ is a voxel survival-sandbox game that challenges players to better manage their time through automation. Explore a vast, procedurally generated world, gather resources, and build your own pipelines (through .. well pipes, machinery, time-shared terminals, and programmable toad-shaped androids called 'toids') as we attempt to survive & thrive on flat-earth.
